ABOUT ME

I’m a junior software developer with a non-traditional background and a practical, people-first approach to building software. After working in music therapy, I transitioned into tech, bringing strong communication skills, structured problem-solving, and experience working closely with stakeholders. I’ve since trained through intensive, project-based learning and work confidently across the full stack, with a particular strength in frontend development using React, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside backend work in Django and REST APIs. I’ve contributed to backend development, fixed frontend bugs, written supporting scripts, and helped deliver projects from requirements through to deployment. I’m adaptable, quick to learn, and comfortable stepping in wherever I’m needed. I’m now looking for a junior role where I can add value, grow alongside experienced developers, and build software that’s genuinely useful.
